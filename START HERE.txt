Tintin's AI Chess Analysis — START HERE
=======================================

Analyze your chess games with Stockfish and get your mistakes explained in
plain words. Pick the ONE option that matches what you want:


🎯  I JUST WANT TO REVIEW MY GAMES  (no terminal needed)
--------------------------------------------------------
  • macOS:    Get "Tintin's AI Chess Analysis.app" from the Releases page,
              drag it into Applications, then right-click it → Open.
              (Releases: https://github.com/Chess-analysis-mcp/tintins-chess-analysis/releases)

  • Windows:  Double-click  "Tintin's AI Chess Analysis.bat"
  • Linux:    Double-click  "Tintin's AI Chess Analysis.command"

  The first launch sets everything up (needs internet once), then opens a
  chess board in your browser. Review a game from anywhere: paste or upload
  a PGN (Chess.com, OTB, any site), or — if you play on Lichess — just enter
  your username and it loads your recent games for you.
  To quit: just close the browser tab.


🤖  I WANT IT INSIDE CLAUDE CODE  (the MCP server, for terminal users)
---------------------------------------------------------------------
  • macOS / Linux:   ./install.sh
  • Windows:         powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File .\install.ps1

  Then reload Claude Code, paste a PGN, and say "analyze this game."


Full details, options, and configuration are in README.md.
